## What is FOM Analytics?

FOM Analytics is an internal platform that consolidates multiple data sources into a single, unified interface. It brings clarity to your operations by combining data into visual analytics and automating the delivery of reports directly to your customers.

This platform is designed to push beyond the limits of Google Sheets by migrating your workflows into a robust internal system built for scale.

## Replacing Google Sheets

FOM Analytics was built to address the limitations of managing data in Google Sheets. By moving your operations into this internal system, you gain:

- Centralized control over all data pipelines
- Reliable, scheduled report automation
- A scalable foundation that grows with your business
